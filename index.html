Our Story

Meet Jennifer Yturriondobeitia

CEO/Founder of YNot Innovators

While working for a large healthcare system to transform providers to a value based or alternative payment model, Jen realized there was an acute, unmet need for healthcare integration and innovation. Fulfilling this need resulted in multiple consults both in and outside of the healthcare system’s provider network to develop disruptive healthcare transformation. This need for disruption and request for expertise was the reason YNI was created.
